KEC Nigeria — Case Study

Knowledge Exchange Centre (KEC) is a non-profit organization based in Lagos. When I joined as Communications Lead in 2022, the brand had a minimal digital footprint and lacked a structured communications strategy.

The Challenge

KEC had a small social media audience of around 370 followers, low engagement, no newsletter presence, and no formal strategy for digital growth or grant communication.

What I Did

I built a full multi-channel communications strategy — covering content, community management, paid advertising, email marketing, and grant writing. I aligned every campaign to KEC's goals, created a consistent brand voice, and produced analytics reports to continuously improve performance.
